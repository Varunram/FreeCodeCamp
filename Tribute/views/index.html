A Brief Timeline of his Life

  • Born on 15 October 1931 to a fisherman family from Rameswaram
  • Sold Newspapers to support his family's income at aa very young age
  • An average student at school but had a huge desire to learn
  • 1954: Graduated from St.Joseph's College Tiruchirapalli
  • 1955: Moved to Madras to study Aerospace Engineering at Madras Institute of Technology
  • 1960: Joined DRDO as a scientist
  • 1964: Visits NASA's facilitied in the United States
  • 1969: Starts working at the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO)
  • 1970-1990: Develops India's highly successful Satellite Launch Program PSLV
  • 1998: Leads India's historic Pokhran-II Test
  • 2002: Became the 11th President of India
  • 2007: Retires from Presidency
  • 2015: The great man passes away on 27 July at 7:45 pm IST
